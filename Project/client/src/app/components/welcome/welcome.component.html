What is the Pedibus

The Pedibus is an initiative to accompany children on foot to school, under the supervision of an adult. Convivial, healthy, economic and ecological, the Pedibus is a life-changing invention!

How does it work?

Like a real bus, the Pedibus respects a schedule, follows a route and has its own drivers. The only difference is that it is a walking bus, organized and managed by the parents.

Our Lines

Our lines cover almost the entire territory of the city of Turin. If other lines are necessary they can be indicated to the administrators.
